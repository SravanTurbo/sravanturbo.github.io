Ten years of building backend systems. This is where I write about how I think. Not what I built.

The posts are about decisions. Why I chose one thing over another, what I got wrong before I got it right, what I'd do differently now. The reasoning behind calls made in production that don't fit anywhere else.

I write this partly so others can learn from it, partly so I can. Putting the reasoning down forces you to find out whether you actually understood it.
